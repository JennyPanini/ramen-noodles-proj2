Cook Tam Soup

Weight: 450g

Year of Release: 2020

Rating: 3.1/5

Notable flavors: beef; ribs, soft head meat, light brisket meat

This product is honestly alight. There isn't much special to it other than the strong beef flavor. It's inspired by gomtang, or beef bone soup. It's alright to eat this if I had no other option, but this isn't something that I would enjoy eating so often.
