Kimchi Ramyun

Weight: 120g

Year of Release: 2007

Rating: 3/5

Notable flavors: kimchi

It tastes very similar to the OG Shin ramyun. Since it's a product targeted to overseas consumers, it was less spicy for me than the OG. Other than the enhanced kimchi flavor, I didn't really find anything special with this product and thought it was pretty forgettable (especially I can name at least eight better Nongshim products than this one).
