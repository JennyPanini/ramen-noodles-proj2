Pawn Snack

Weight: 85g

Year of Release: 2018

Rating: 4.5/5

Notable flavors: Shin ramyun

This is a very cute snack! My sister especially loves this snack, and while it's basically dried ramyun compressed into bite-sized balls, it's very tasty. It's almost nostalgic as well, as it reminds me of times when I was young and couldn't use the stove by myself, so while my mom made instant ramyun, I'd take the bag and eat the leftover dried noodles that broke apart from the compressed package.
