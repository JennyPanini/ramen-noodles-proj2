Nongshim Sarimyun Noodle

Weight: 110g

Year of Release: 2016

Rating: 2.9/5

Notable flavors: chewy noodles (good for jigae and jeongol)

In comparison to the Nongshim Rice Noodle product, I was slightly disappointed by the fact that it's slightly more bland, but the noodles taste better. They're chewier and have more texture (if that is even the right word to say). I first heard of this product from my dad, who bought it at a local H-Mart and wanted to be adventurous. While he told me it tasted okay, he never tried it again (and stopped being adventurous after that).
