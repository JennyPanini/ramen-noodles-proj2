Nongshim Rice Noodle

Weight: 73g

Year of Release: 2019

Rating: 3/5

Notable flavors: rice, chicken broth, egg garnish flakes, green onion, red pepper

This product surprised me with the taste. I thought it was going to taste bland, but there was actually more flavor than I thought. Not exactly a favorite, but it's worth a try. My cousin told me about this product; he ate this in the middle of the night during uni finals and said it's good for temporarily starving off hunger, but it's not as good as spicer Nongshim products.
