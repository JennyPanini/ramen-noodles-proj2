Neogubonara

Weight: 130g

Year of Release: 2017

Rating: 4.6/5

Notable flavors: creamy carbonara sauce, fish cake

This tasted a lot better than I previously expected. My cousin tried this when he was on vacation in Korea and bought one for me to eat. I really like the creamy carbonara sauce with the racoon-shaped rice cakes. It's a very different texture compared to other Nongshim products, and one of the few with a thicker sauce/broth. Overall, highly recommend if you're feeling daring and want to go out of your comfort zone. Just a warning, though, it's very spicy! When I tried this, I had a gallon of milk next to me.
