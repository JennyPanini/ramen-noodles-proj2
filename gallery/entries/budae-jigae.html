Budae Jigae

Weight: 127g

Year of Release: 2016

Rating: 4.5/5

Notable flavors: ham, fish cake, green onions

This is an instant version of what is called "Korean Army Stew". It's one of my favorite Korean foods due to its spiciness and the inclusion of fish cakes and ham (and sometimes spam when my mom makes it). However, I enjoyed this version as well. There's a lot of flavor to it, and might even be better than the OG Shin ramyun! The noodles are chewy and soft as well.
