Pizza Snack

Weight: unknown

Year of Release: 1984

Rating: 1.5/5

Notable flavors: Italian pizza and American barbeque

This is an unusual Nongshim product, similar to Kimchi Ramyun since it tastes like a product marketed to overseas customers and/or made with Western influence in mind. Unfortunately, this product does not hit the mark; the taste of pizze in this snack felt weird to me and too artificial. As an American, this is not a product I would recommend.
