Cook Tam Rabokki

Weight: 390g

Year of Release: 2018

Rating: 4/5

Notable flavors: chewy rice cake, sarimyun noodle, sliced fish cake, fermented red pepper paste

Initially, I didn't really like tteokbokki so I didn't like this dish as much. But after spending time in NY and eating lots of poultry and rice and other non-spicy foods, I ate this recently and really liked it. It's definitely very spicy, and both noodles and rice cake are very chewy.
