Angry Chapaguri

Weight: 140g

Year of Release: 2021

Rating: 1.5/5

Notable flavors: chapaghetti (chajang ramen) and neoguri (thick, chewy noodles)

My sister likes chapaguri, I don't. She likes that it's savory and spicy and basically ramen with a twist; it also helps that ‘Korean Beef Sirloin Chapaguri’ was introduced in 2019 film ‘Parasite’ by Director Bong Joon-ho, a movie that we both liked. However, as much as I tried to like this product, I wasn't able to finish it and gave the rest to my sister.
